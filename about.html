About Cook Easy


Cook Easy is a project from young people to young people, that need help in the kitchen.

If it is because you just moved from home, or never really have been in a kitchen before. Or if you are a student with tiny space and few ingrediens, then we have the recipes for you, that are super easy to follow.

We made this project because vi wants it to be easy for young people to learn to cook and bake, that is easy when you are new in the kitchen.

We did this because we know ourself how easy it is to just buy som fast food, that not always is the healthiest, and some times even more expensive compared to if you made the food yourself. It also gives you satisfaction when you make it yourself.
