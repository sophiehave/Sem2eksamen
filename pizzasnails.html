Pizzasnails

approximately 20 pizzasnails




Ingrediens


The dough
  • 50g yeast
  • 3 dl water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 table spoon oil
  • 500g pizza flour (or ordinary if you don't have pizza flour)


    • Topping
  • 1 can tomato puree
  • 100g chopped ham
  • 100g mozzarella
  • A little salt and pepper if wanted

    • How to


    Approximately 20 min. at 200° - traditionel oven.

    1. Place the yeast i a big bowl and add the water. Stir until the yeast is dissolved.

    2. Add salt and oil.

    3. Stir ¾ of the flour in with a spoon.

    4. Take the rest of the dough on a table with the rest of the flour, and knead the rest of the flour in. Knead it well. Roll it out into a square. (approximately 35 x 40 cm).

    5. Stir tomato puree, salt and pepper together. Spred out the tomato puree, salt and pepper, and add the ham and cheese.

    6. Rool the dough together to a roulade. Push the roulade gently together so it measures approximately 40 cm. Cut it into 20 pizzasnails.

    7. Place the snails on a baking tray with paper and let them raise covered for 20 minuts.

    8. Bake the snails i the oven.

    9. After the 20 minuts, take them out of the ovn, and enjoy.
