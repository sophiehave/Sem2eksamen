Oatmealballs

approximately 30 oatmealballs




Ingrediens


  • 175g (approximately 5¾ dl) fine rolled oats
  • 100g (approximately 2 dl) powdered sugar
  • 4½ table spoon (approximately 25g) cocoa powder
  • 100g soft butter
  • 2 table spoon water (or coffee)

    • How to


    1. Mix the dry ingrediens in a bowl.

    2. Stir the soft buffer in the mixture and knead it together. Add water and knead again.

    3. Roll the mixture into small balls, and roll them in coconut flour, powdered sugar and/or cocoa powder. If you want, you can roll the balls in your hands, it will let the decoration stick better.

    4. Place the oatmealballs covered in the fridge, ready for serving.
