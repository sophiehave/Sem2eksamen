Banana pancakes

4 pancakes




Ingrediens


  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bananas

    • How to


    1. Blend the eggs and the bananas together to a combined soild. You can use a blender, or blend it yourself, if you don't have a blender.

    2. Warm a pan, and place some oil or butter on the pan.

    3. Place the wanted amount of dough, and turn he pancakes when it starts to bobble.

    4. Repeat with the rest of the dough.

    5. Enjoy your pancakes.
