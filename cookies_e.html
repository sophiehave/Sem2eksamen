Cookies width chocolate chips

approximately 30 cookies




Ingrediens


  • 150g butter
  • 4½ dl brown sugar
  • ¾ dl sugar
  • 1 tea spoon vanilla sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 6½ dl oats
  • 3 dl flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tea spoon baking soda
  • 200g dark chocolate (or 100g dark and 100g white)

    • How to


    1. Melt the butter og stir it with brown sugar, sugar and vanilla sugar till it turns creamy.

    2. Whip the eggs lightly and stir it together with the oats. Mix the flour with salt and baking soda and knead it in the dough slowly.

    3. Chop the chocolate in cubes, and knead it into the dough.

    4. Shape balls in your hands and press them flat on a baking tray with paper.

    5. Bake them at 180C for 8-12 minuts depending on the size.

    6. Place afterwards the paper on a rack, and let the cookis cool.
