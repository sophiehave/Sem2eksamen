Pasta with tomatoes and mozzarella

4 people




Ingrediens


  • 400g pasta
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 15 cherry tomatoes
  • 125g mozzarella
  • 1 handfull fesh basil, chopped finely

    • How to


    1. Cook the pasta in water with salt and oil. Follow the intructions on the packaging for how long the pasta needs.

    2. Meanwhile the pasta cooks, place the chopped tomatoes in a pot with middelheat.

    3. When the pasta is finnished, place it in a dish with the tomatoes, mozzarella and basil.
